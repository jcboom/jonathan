overview & impact
When I began Circle Innovations the Internet looked like it could fizzle out at any moment. Stagnant, sluggish, and inactive web pages made up a landscape that was scattered among a vast Primordial Ocean of inter(web)space. Truly exciting websites existed like treasures waiting to be found on new pristine islands in a mysterious new world. Macromedia (now Adobe) Flash was a glimmer of what movement and interaction the Internet could be. Back in those days when the Internet had a little “i”, a "onmouseover" effect or what we know now know as a “hover,” was a costly JavaScript endeavor. It was during those days when I crouched over my keyboard fixated on variables and functions for hours on end that I founded a web development firm together with Armando Bravo and Luis Rivera. I didn't possess a formal education or professional training. I haxored my way. Every step was a hacking effort.
